Dive into Wonder

World's Largest Suspended Aquarium

Immerse yourself in a mesmerising underwater world at Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo, one of the largest suspended aquariums on the planet! Journey beneath the surface and discover over 140 species and 300+ majestic sharks and rays, all housed within a 10-million-litre tank located on the ground floor of Dubai Mall.

Subaquatic Tunnel:
Embark on a 48-metre underwater adventure through a transparent tunnel, offering a 270-degree acrylic walkthrough tunnel from 11 metres below the surface, while sharks, rays and a kaleidoscope of marine life glide around you.
Beneath Your Feet:
Take a glass-bottom boat ride and watch the underwater world unfold directly beneath you.
Beneath The Sea

Unveiling Ocean Mysteries

Add a stunning new dimension to your experience at Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo by entering the larger-than-life aquarium tunnel. Step into the diversity of the open ocean as you walk through this extraordinary 48-metre-long tunnel, 11 metres below the surface. Watch colourful fish dart between coral formations and witness rays glide gracefully overhead.

Walk Amongst Marine Life:
Feel the magic as you become part of this underwater world, surrounded by a vibrant tapestry of fish and rays.
Witness the Feeding Frenzy:
Witness a thrilling spectacle as skilled divers engage in feeding sessions with sharks and rays, offering a glimpse into their fascinating feeding behaviours.
Discover Tranquillity in the Deep:
Immerse yourself in the peaceful ambiance of the tunnel. Escape the everyday as you observe the movements of the underwater world.

Amazing Facts

Did You Know?

Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo holds several records and fascinating facts that make it one of the most impressive aquatic attractions in the world.

World's Largest Viewing Panel:
Our 10-million litre tank features one of the world's largest acrylic viewing panels, measuring 32.88 meters wide and 8.3 meters tall.
Home to 33,000+ Animals:
We house over 33,000 aquatic animals representing more than 140 species, including over 300 sharks and rays combined.
Meet King Croc:
Our famous King Croc weighs over 750kg and is over 40 years old - one of the largest crocodiles in captivity.
