The Green Planet

Step into Dubai's unique tropical bio-dome and experience the enchantment of over 3,000 plants and animals, this bio-dome recreates the magical atmosphere of a real rainforest, complete with free-flying birds, fascinating reptiles, and exotic flora from around the world.

Canopy Walk:

Experience the rainforest from above as you walk through the canopy on elevated walkways, getting up close with tropical birds and discovering the secrets of the forest crown.

Wildlife Encounters:

Meet amazing creatures including colorful toucans, playful sloths, exotic snakes, and vibrant butterflies in their naturalistic habitat.

Educational Journey:

Learn about rainforest ecosystems, conservation efforts, and the importance of biodiversity through interactive displays and guided experiences.

Our Mission

Conservation Through Education

At The Green Planet, we believe in the power of education to drive conservation. Our bio-dome serves as a living classroom, inspiring visitors to understand and protect the world's precious rainforests.

Breeding Programs:
We participate in international breeding programs for endangered species, contributing to global conservation efforts.
Educational Workshops:
Regular workshops and guided tours help visitors understand the critical role rainforests play in our planet's health.
Sustainability Initiatives:
Learn about sustainable practices and how small actions can make a big difference in protecting our environment.

Plan Your Visit

Essential Information

Make the most of your rainforest adventure with these helpful tips and information about visiting The Green Planet.

Best Time to Visit:
Early mornings offer the best animal activity, while afternoons provide optimal lighting for photography enthusiasts.
What to Bring:
Comfortable walking shoes, a camera, and a sense of adventure. The bio-dome maintains tropical conditions year-round.
Duration:
Allow 2-3 hours to fully explore all four levels and enjoy the various animal encounters and educational exhibits.
