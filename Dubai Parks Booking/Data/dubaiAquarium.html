Amazing Facts

Did You Know?

Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo holds several records and fascinating facts that make it one of the most impressive aquatic attractions in the world. From engineering marvels to conservation successes, discover what makes us truly unique.

World Records:
Our 10-million litre tank is one of the largest suspended aquariums in the world, featuring the largest acrylic panel according to Guinness World Records.
Marine Diversity:
We're home to over 33,000 aquatic animals representing more than 140 species, including over 300 sharks and rays combined.
Engineering Marvel:
The aquarium panel is 32.88 meters wide, 8.3 meters tall, and 750mm thick - weighing more than 245 tonnes!
King Croc:
Meet our famous King Croc who weighs over 750kg and is over 40 years old - one of the largest crocodiles in captivity.

Sustainability Initiatives

Protecting Our Oceans

We're committed to environmental sustainability and ocean conservation. Through various initiatives and partnerships, we work to reduce our environmental impact while educating visitors about the importance of protecting marine ecosystems.

Plastic-Free Zone:
We've eliminated single-use plastics from our facility, using eco-friendly alternatives and encouraging visitors to join our mission.
Coral Restoration:
Our coral propagation program has successfully grown and transplanted over 1,000 coral fragments, contributing to reef restoration in the Arabian Gulf.
Energy Conservation:
Using state-of-the-art LED lighting and energy-efficient life support systems, we've reduced our energy consumption by 40% over the past five years.
Community Outreach:
Through beach cleanups, school programs, and public awareness campaigns, we engage over 50,000 people annually in marine conservation efforts.
