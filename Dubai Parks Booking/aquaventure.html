Ultimate Water Adventure

Aquaventure Waterpark

Experience the ultimate aquatic adventure at Aquaventure Waterpark, the largest waterpark in the Middle East and Europe! With over 105 slides, attractions, and experiences, this waterpark offers endless thrills for all ages. From record-breaking slides to marine encounters, prepare for an unforgettable day of splash-tastic fun.

Record-Breaking Slides:
Brave the world's largest waterslide - the Odyssey of Terror, featuring a 6-story drop and heart-pounding speeds that will leave you breathless.
Private Beach Access:
Relax on 1 kilometer of pristine private beach with crystal-clear waters of the Arabian Gulf, perfect for swimming and water sports.
Thrilling Attractions

Adrenaline-Pumping Adventures

Get your heart racing with our collection of world-class water slides and attractions. From high-speed racing slides to gravity-defying drops, Aquaventure offers the most thrilling water experiences in the region.

The Leap of Faith:
Take the ultimate plunge down a near-vertical 9-story slide that sends you through a clear tunnel surrounded by sharks and rays.
Aquaconda:
Experience the world's largest water slide by volume as you navigate through twists, turns, and surprises in this mammoth tube slide.
Tower of Neptune:
Choose from multiple slides offering different levels of thrills, from family-friendly fun to extreme adventures for the bravest souls.
Marine Encounters

Up Close with Sea Life

Dive into incredible marine experiences where you can interact with amazing sea creatures. From swimming with dolphins to walking with sharks, create unforgettable memories with the ocean's most fascinating inhabitants.

Dolphin Bay:
Swim, play, and interact with friendly bottlenose dolphins in their natural lagoon habitat in this once-in-a-lifetime experience.
Shark Safari:
Snorkel through the Ambassador Lagoon and come face-to-face with over 65,000 marine animals including sharks, rays, and tropical fish.
Sea Lion Point:
Meet playful sea lions up close and learn about these intelligent marine mammals from expert marine biologists.
Family Fun

Adventures for Everyone

Aquaventure isn't just about extreme thrills - we offer plenty of family-friendly attractions and experiences that everyone can enjoy together, from toddlers to grandparents.

Splashers Lagoon:
A dedicated children's play area with mini slides, water cannons, and interactive features designed specifically for little adventurers.
Lazy River:
Float along the relaxing 2.3-kilometer lazy river that winds through lush landscapes and offers a peaceful break from the excitement.
Rainbow Reef:
Snorkel in this colorful lagoon filled with 20,000 tropical fish from around the world - perfect for first-time snorkelers.
