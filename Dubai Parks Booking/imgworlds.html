IMG Worlds of Adventure

Enter a world of imagination and adventure at IMG Worlds of Adventure, the planet's largest indoor theme park! Spanning 1.5 million square feet, this incredible destination brings your favorite cartoon and superhero characters to life through cutting-edge technology and heart-stopping attractions.

Marvel Zone:
Join forces with legendary superheroes including Spider-Man, The Avengers, and Thor in action-packed adventures that will test your courage and save the world.
Cartoon Network Zone:
Step into the colorful world of your favorite Cartoon Network characters including Ben 10, The Powerpuff Girls, and Adventure Time in family-friendly attractions.
Themed Zones

Four Incredible Worlds

Explore four distinct themed zones, each offering unique adventures and experiences. From superhero action to cartoon fun, prehistoric adventures to shopping and dining, IMG Worlds has something for everyone.

Marvel Zone:
Team up with Spider-Man, The Avengers, and other Marvel heroes in thrilling rides and immersive experiences that bring comic book action to life.
Cartoon Network Zone:
Enter the vibrant world of Ben 10, The Powerpuff Girls, and Adventure Time with family-friendly attractions and interactive experiences.
Lost Valley - Dinosaur Adventure:
Journey back 65 million years to encounter realistic animatronic dinosaurs and experience prehistoric adventures in this thrilling zone.
Rides & Attractions

Thrills for All Ages

Experience over 20 rides and attractions ranging from gentle family experiences to extreme thrill rides. Our state-of-the-art attractions use the latest technology to create unforgettable adventures.

The Avengers: Battle of Ultron:
Join the Avengers in this immersive 3D dark ride as you battle Ultron's army using cutting-edge motion simulation technology.
The Haunted Hotel:
Brave one of the world's largest and most terrifying haunted attractions, featuring live actors and spine-chilling special effects.
Predator:
Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush on this intense spinning coaster that reaches speeds of up to 60 km/h with multiple inversions.
Family Fun

Entertainment & Dining

Beyond the thrilling rides, IMG Worlds offers world-class dining, shopping, and entertainment options. Enjoy character meet-and-greets, live shows, and themed restaurants.

IMG Boulevard:
Shop, dine, and be entertained in this vibrant zone featuring themed restaurants, retail stores, and the spectacular IMG Theatre.
Character Meet & Greets:
Meet your favorite Marvel superheroes and Cartoon Network characters for photos and autographs throughout the day.
Climate-Controlled Comfort:
Enjoy perfect weather year-round in our fully air-conditioned environment, making it the ideal destination regardless of outside conditions.
